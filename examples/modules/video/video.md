# Video Module Examples

This folder contains examples for working with the controller's video capabilities.


## What's Here

- [**single_camera.py**](single_camera.py) — Basic example showing how to access frames from the Go2's internal camera 
- [**multi_camera.py**](multi_camera.py) — Access multiple cameras at once: a depth data from a RealSense D34i depth camera and the Go2's internal camera
- [**streaming.py**](streaming.py) — Stream the camera feed over WebRTC so you can view it in a browser on your local network
